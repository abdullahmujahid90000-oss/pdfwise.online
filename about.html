About PDFWise

Free PDF tools built for everyone — with privacy and simplicity at the core.

Our Story

PDFWise was created out of frustration. Every time we needed to quickly compress a PDF, merge a couple of files, or convert a document, we'd run into the same wall: free tools with annoying limits, forced signups, watermarks on every download, and constant upsell prompts.

We knew the technology to do all of this existed in modern browsers. So we built PDFWise — a suite of PDF tools that runs entirely in your browser, with no server uploads, no accounts, and no catches. Just tools that work.

Today, PDFWise offers 20+ PDF tools used by students, professionals, small business owners, and anyone who works with documents. Our goal is simple: make professional-grade PDF tools accessible to everyone around the world, completely free.

How We're Different

Most PDF websites share the same business model: offer limited free features and push users toward paid plans. They compress files only up to 5MB, add watermarks unless you pay, and require you to create an account just to download your own file.

PDFWise does the opposite. We use open-source JavaScript libraries (pdf-lib.js and pdf.js) to process PDFs directly in your browser. This means:

  • No file size limits — your browser handles processing, not our servers
  • No privacy concerns — we literally cannot see your files
  • No waiting — processing happens at your device's speed
  • No account — there's nothing to track, so no need to identify you

We monetize through advertising, not your data or your patience.
